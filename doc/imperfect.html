(inner) solidBlock(minimCount, possibleDivisors)

Source:
  • interpreter/imperfection.js, line 219

Returns true if a duration (in minims) is 3, 6 or 9 times the length of any of a list of candidate values, otherwise returns false. This is for durations of windows of notes, to see whether they represent a coherent block of whole mensural units (which is likely to imply that they fall on the beat and that they don't imperfect their neighbours).
