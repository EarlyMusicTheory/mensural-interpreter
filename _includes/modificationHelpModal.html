Help

To run the complete interpreter analysis, click on "Run" ("Basic") will only run the beat independent analysis.

"Blocks" will show you the detected mensurally coherent blocks.

To inspect the interpreter analysis, click on a note within the rendered music.
You may navigate (within bar lines) with ← and →.

Modified notes will be highlighted by a color code in interpreter mode:

  • perfecta
  • imperfecta
  • altera
  • dot of augmentation
  • partial or non-standard imperfection

In instructor mode, color codes give feedback on the results.
